Open position

I am looking for one self-motived Ph.D. student who is interested in software engineering research! The Ph.D. position is fully funded and the student will work in the Software Science Group (SWS) of the Institute for Computing and Information Sciences (iCIS) at Radboud University . Please refer to https://www.ru.nl/phd/ for the general information about Ph.D. candidates at the university.


The research topics are not predefined. However, they shall fall into the scope of software engineering. You can come up with your own ideas and work on the projects I provide. We will define the topic together. At the end of the Ph.D., you are supposed to become an independent researcher.


What I Can Offer
  • A reasonable salary with holiday allowance and end-of-year bonus
  • Flexible working hours + other employee benefits provided by Radboud University
  • An opportunity to conduct cool research with cool people internationally

Requirements
  • A Master's degree in computer science/software engineering
  • Good programming skills
  • Good English skills
  • Strong interest in software engineering
  • An open mind

How to Apply

If you are interested, please send your CV and transcripts to bin.lin [at] ru.nl. If you have any questions, please feel free to let me know!
