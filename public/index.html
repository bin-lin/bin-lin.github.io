LIN, Bin

I'm currently living in Eindhoven, the Netherlands. I was born and raised in Wenzhou, China in December 1992. I also spent four years in Guangzhou, China during my undergraduation from 2010 to 2014.

Education

From September 2014 I'm a graduate student majoring in Computer Science and Engineering at Eindhoven University of Technology, the Netherlands. I'm also a visiting student of the CHISEL group at University of Victoria, Canada from September to November 2015. I got my bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at South China University of Technology in China. My bachelor's graduation project was an object detection and tracking system for video surveillance developed with C++, QT and OpenCV.

Fields of Interest

My interests span a wide range of fields in software engineering and web engineering. Specific topics include social aspects of software engineering and mining software repositories. I'll do my master's graducation project at SET group under the supervision of Alexander Serebrenik (if everything goes well ;-). As a student of web science stream at TU/e, I am also interested in web information retrieval and data mining. I also develop web applications with Django, Flask, SQL and NoSQL databases, ElasticSearch, etc.
